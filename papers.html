Workshop Schedule (NEW)

We are so excited to be a part of IEEE Visualization community for the third time. This will be the first hybrid TREX, and we are planning to engage both in-person and virtual attendees.
Hybrid Breakout Session Discussions: For the first time, we are planning a breakout discussion where the attendees would get a chance to connect with one another and do a group discussion activity. We will summarize and discuss group findings during the workshop closing. The Zoom links for virtual attendees to join will be announced during the workshop by the organizers. To access the workshop, you must be registered for the conference via IEEE VIS Website.
Schedule: The workshop will take place on Sunday, October 24 from 9:00 am to 12:00 pm (US Central Time) - Room: Oklahoma Station 8.
